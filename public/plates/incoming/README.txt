Drop white/light blank garment photos here, then run:

  npx tsx scripts/ingest-plate.ts --in public/plates/incoming/<file> --garment <type> --view front --color white --bg auto

See PLATE_GUIDE.md at the repo root for shoot specs and options.
One photo per garment SHAPE (tshirt, longsleeve, tank, polo, hoodie...) — recolor handles every color.
